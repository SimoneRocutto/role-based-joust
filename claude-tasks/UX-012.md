# UX-012: Role-based — role visibility on dashboard (NOT phone)

**Priority**: P2 | **Status**: TODO | **Tags**: `NEEDS-INPUT`

*Includes former UX-014 (show roles on dashboard player cards) — merged because same question.*

## CRITICAL CONSTRAINT: Phones face OPPONENTS

Phones are chest-mounted, screen facing outward. The player CANNOT see their own screen. Other players CAN. Therefore:

- **Role names MUST NOT appear on the phone screen.** Showing "NINJA" on a player's chest reveals their secret role to everyone in the room.
- The phone screen is for opponents, not the player. Think of it as a jersey/badge.

## Problem

In role-based mode, the player's role is shown only during the brief role reveal sequence (audio + screen flash). After that, there's no persistent reminder anywhere. The audit flagged this as a concern on the phone — but that's wrong (phones face opponents). The real question is whether roles should be shown on the **dashboard** for spectators.

## Open question (NEEDS-INPUT)

**This is a gameplay design decision, not just a UI task.**

Two sub-questions:

### 1. Should roles be visible on the dashboard?

1. **Always visible** — roles shown on player cards from round start. Spectators follow the drama ("the Vampire is low HP!"). Most spectator-friendly.
2. **Revealed on death** — hidden during gameplay, revealed when a player dies. Adds mystery, dramatic reveals.
3. **Never visible on dashboard** — roles stay secret from spectators too. Only communicated via TTS audio.
4. **Configurable** — admin toggle. Safest option.

### 2. How should the player remember their own role?

Since the player can't see their phone:
1. **Audio only** — TTS through earbuds during role reveal is enough. Players must remember.
2. **Audio reinforcement** — periodic TTS reminders ("You are the Ninja") through earbuds.
3. **No extra help** — the initial reveal is enough. Adds tension and is simpler.

## Implementation plan (after input)

- If dashboard roles: add role name/icon to dashboard player card component (conditionally based on visibility setting).
- If audio reinforcement: add periodic TTS reminder via the audio system.
- May need a `revealRoles` setting if configurable.

## Dependencies

UX-003 (dashboard card redesign for team modes) — roles could be part of a redesigned card.

## Estimated scope

Small if "always visible on dashboard" — just add a label to cards.
Medium if configurable or if audio reinforcement is added.
