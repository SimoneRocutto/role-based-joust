# GM-003: Classic — trophy on round winner, fix skull logic

**Priority**: P2 | **Status**: DONE

## Problem

From the user's TODO.md: "classic at the end of the round shows skull on every player that is not the first one with most total points. Trophy should be shown on round winner."

Currently, at round end, skulls appear on all players except the one with the most total points. But the round winner (last alive) should get a trophy indicator, and skulls should only show on eliminated players.

## Affected modes

classic only

## Source

User's TODO.md — short term priority

## Implementation plan

1. **Client dashboard**: At round end, show trophy/crown icon on the round winner (last alive / most round points)
   - Distinguish between "round winner" (trophy) and "eliminated this round" (skull)
   - Players who survived but didn't win should show neither trophy nor skull
2. **Server**: Verify `round:end` payload identifies the round winner clearly.
3. **Tests**: Unit test round-end display logic for various scenarios.

## Dependencies

UX-005 (round-end summary overlay) — the trophy display should be part of the round-end summary design.

## Estimated scope

Small — conditional icon display based on existing round-end data.
