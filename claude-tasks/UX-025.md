# UX-025: Team modes: champion status bar should show team name

**Priority:** P2
**Status:** TODO
**Tags:** cross-mode, team

## Problem

The game-over dashboard status bar reads "Bot 1 is the champion!" in team modes. The winner is the team, not an individual player. This contradicts the team leaderboard shown above.

Flagged in: classic-team, death-count-team, domination.

## Proposed fix

When teams are enabled, the champion status bar message should read "Blue Team is the champion!" (or "Red Team wins!") instead of naming an individual player.

## Files likely affected

- `server/src/server.ts` or wherever the champion message is composed
- Possibly `server/src/sockets/helpers.ts`

## Notes

Simple text change but important for team mode coherence.
