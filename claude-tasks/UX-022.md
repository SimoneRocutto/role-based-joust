# UX-022: Dashboard team modes — HP not visible on player cards

**Priority**: P1 | **Status**: DONE

## Problem

During active gameplay in domination (team mode), the dashboard player cards show no visible HP change even when players have taken significant damage (50% and 25% HP). The full-HP and damaged screenshots look identical. Spectators cannot tell who is hurt until someone dies.

In solo modes, cards use border color (green -> yellow -> red) to indicate HP. In team modes, the border color may be overridden by team color, removing the HP signal.

## Affected modes

All team modes: domination, classic-team, death-count-team, long-live-the-king

## Audit references

- Domination: `05_dash_active.png` vs `05b_dash_active_damaged.png` — R-D04

## Solution

Team mode player cards need an HP indicator that doesn't conflict with team colors. Options:
1. **HP bar inside the card** — a thin horizontal bar at the bottom or top of each card, color-coded green/yellow/red
2. **Background opacity/shade** — card background darkens as HP drops
3. **Numeric HP** — show HP percentage on each card
4. **Inner glow/border** — use a secondary inner border for HP while keeping outer border for team color

Option 1 (HP bar) is probably cleanest — it's a separate visual channel from the team border color.

## Implementation plan

1. **Client dashboard**: Add an HP bar or indicator to the team-mode player card component.
2. Ensure it works alongside team color borders without visual conflict.
3. **Tests**: Unit test HP indicator renders at various HP levels in team mode.
4. **Screenshot**: Verify with damaged state screenshots.

## Dependencies

UX-003 (DONE — team mode uses full player cards)

## Estimated scope

Small-medium — add HP indicator to player card component for team modes.
